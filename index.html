NIGERIA DECIDES

Electoral Reform

The biggest threat to our democracy took place yesterday right under our noses. Where innocent citizens were coerced, threatened, and violently deterred from exercising their civic duties. This isnt an exhaustive list, it's a start;


  1. Ballot Paper and boxes re-design/optimized process. We saw too many invalids.snatched, destroyed , torn ballots.
  2. Voting Equipments
  3. Counting Procedures
  4. Bribery/Combat vote buying
  5. Voter safety
  6. Voter Eligibility
  7. Balanced Zoning - some polling zones were completely overwhelmed with 4000+ people where nearby zones had 100.
  8. Candidate financing/transparency - we need a declaration of the sources of income to finance campaaigns. A candidate may not borrow to finance their campaign. Nigerians won't pay for it later.
  9. Conflict of interest
  10. Real-time Results
